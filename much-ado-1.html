Midterm Description, Wireframe, and Site Map

My midterm project is an interactive timeline of the show I'm currently directing, Much Ado About Nothing. Users are able to click through chronologically or move to a specific part of the rehearsal process, learning about the cast, how we modified the script for this production, playing some Shakespeare trivia, and seeing the final product! (Though I won't be able to embed that until next weekend, when we actually perform.)
